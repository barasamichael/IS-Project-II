The Settlement Challenge

International students face unique obstacles when settling in Nairobi. These challenges create unnecessary stress and can impact academic performance and personal wellbeing.

Fragmented Information

Students waste valuable time visiting multiple offices, checking outdated websites, and relying on inconsistent peer advice to gather basic settlement information.

Cultural Navigation

Understanding local systems, terminology, and processes becomes challenging without proper guidance, leading to confusion and delays.

Time Pressures

Critical decisions about accommodation and documentation must be made quickly, but students lack access to timely, reliable guidance when they need it most.

Financial Risks

Incomplete information leads to costly mistakes - from unfavorable rental agreements to missed deadlines that result in additional fees.
