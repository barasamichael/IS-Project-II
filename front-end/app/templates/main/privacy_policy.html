1.

Introduction

SettleBot ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our AI-powered settlement assistant service designed for international students in Nairobi, Kenya.

By using SettleBot, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use our service.

Key Privacy Principles:

We only collect information necessary to provide our service, we never sell your personal data to third parties, we use industry-standard security measures, and we give you control over your information.

2.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our settlement assistance service:

Information You Provide Directly

Data Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Full name, username, email address, password Account creation, authentication, communication
Profile Information University, study program, budget preferences Personalized settlement guidance
Conversations Questions asked, AI responses, chat history Service provision, AI improvement
Feedback Service ratings, bug reports, suggestions Service improvement, issue resolution

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, device type
  • Log Information: IP address, access times, error logs, performance data
  • Location Data: General location information (city/country level only)
  • Performance Data: Response times, system errors, feature usage patterns

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • Sensitive personal information (passport numbers, financial details, medical records)
  • Precise location or GPS coordinates
  • Information from other apps or websites without your permission
  • Voice recordings or camera access
  • Contact lists or personal files from your device
3.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

Primary Service Functions

  • Provide Settlement Guidance: Answer your questions about accommodation, admin processes, and services in Nairobi
  • Personalize Responses: Tailor AI responses based on your university, budget, and preferences
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, authenticate users, provide customer support
  • Communication: Send important service updates, respond to inquiries, provide customer support

Service Improvement

  • AI Training: Analyze conversations to improve our AI assistant's accuracy and helpfulness
  • Feature Development: Understand user needs to develop new features and services
  • Performance Optimization: Monitor and improve system performance, fix bugs, enhance security
  • Research: Conduct research to better understand international student settlement challenges

Legal and Security

  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Terms Enforcement: Enforce our Terms and Conditions and prevent misuse
4.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our service:

  • Cloud hosting providers for data storage and processing
  • Email service providers for account notifications
  • Analytics services for understanding user behavior
  • Security services for fraud prevention and protection

All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and can only use your information for specified purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect and defend our rights or property
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing
  • Protect the personal safety of users or the public

Business Transfers

If SettleBot is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Aggregated Information

We may share aggregated, anonymized information that cannot identify you personally for research, analysis, or business purposes. For example, we might share general usage statistics or trends in settlement questions.

5.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Data Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your personal information
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security systems and software
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and unusual activity

Organizational Safeguards

  • Employee Training: All team members receive privacy and security training
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to personal information
  • Confidentiality Agreements: All employees and contractors sign confidentiality agreements
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and assessments

Your Role in Security:

Help us protect your account by using a strong, unique password, not sharing your login credentials, logging out from shared devices, and reporting any suspicious activity to us immediately.

6.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

Data Retention Timeline

Active Account

While your account is active, we retain your profile information and conversation history to provide personalized service and improve our AI.

Inactive Accounts (2 Years)

If you don't use SettleBot for 2 consecutive years, we'll send you notice before deleting your account and personal information.

Account Deletion

When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Aggregated Data

Anonymized, aggregated data used for research and service improvement may be retained indefinitely as it cannot identify you personally.

Some information may be retained longer if required by law, for legal proceedings, or to resolve disputes.

Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information. Here's what you can do:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of the personal information we have about you, including your conversation history and profile data.

Correct Information

Update or correct any inaccurate personal information through your account settings or by contacting us.

Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal and operational limitations.

Data Portability

Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format so you can transfer it to another service.

Limit Processing

Request that we limit how we process your information in certain circumstances.

Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time, though this may limit service functionality.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  • Use the privacy controls in your account settings
  • Contact us at support.team@jisortublow.co.ke
  • Submit a request through our contact form

We will respond to your requests within 30 days and may need to verify your identity to protect your privacy.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration

11.

Children's Privacy

SettleBot is primarily designed for adult international students (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 without parental consent.

Age Verification

While we don't actively verify users' ages, we ask users to confirm they are 18 or older during registration. If you are under 18, you may only use our service with parental or guardian permission and supervision.

Parental Rights

If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18 without proper consent, we will:

  • Delete the information as quickly as possible
  • Not use the information for any purpose
  • Not share the information with third parties

If you believe we have information about a child under 18, please contact us immediately at support.team@jisortublow.co.ke.
